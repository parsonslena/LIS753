About

Friends of the Palo Alto Library holds a huge sale of more than 50,000 gently used and sometimes new books, CDs, DVDs, VHS, LPs, as well as puzzles, games, and software. Most items have been donated by individuals, estates, and companies in the community; a few are former library books. Everything is sorted and displayed by genre and subject for easy browsing.

When

Every month on the second Saturday and Sunday from 11 a.m. - 4 p.m. list of 2023 dates.

Location

Sale happens in 3 ROOMS at Cubberley Community Center

Donate

Please bring all donations to FOPAL's main Book Sale Room Monday through Saturday from 3 to 5pm (most weeks but not the one before the second Saturday of the month). Full details on how to donate.
