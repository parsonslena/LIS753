About When Location Donate

Check out the monthly used book sale benefitting the Friends of the Palo Alto Library. Shop from more than 10,000 used books, music, magazines, videos, games, toys and more. With new donations every day, the sale is always different and unexpected.

Shop 10 am - 4 pm the first Saturday of every month.

Cubberley Community Center at 4000 Middlefield Road

Donations come from individuals, estates and businesses, as well as items removed from public library circulation. Proceeds benefit Palo Alto Public Library. To learn about special items featured in the upcoming sale, sign up to receive our Book Sale e-Newsletter.
